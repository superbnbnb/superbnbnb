FAQs

The BSCMATRIX game has been designed to be safe, secure and easy to play. For more information and advice please join our Telegram Group

  • WHAT IS BSCMATRIX.COM?

    BSCMATRIX.com is the user interface to the BSCMATRIX smart contract that runs on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC).

    The BSCMATRIX smart contract is a fully decentralised and fully autonomous ~ that is it is not managed or controlled by any single entity. All transactions are mined on BSC and payments made Peer to Peer in a fully decentralised manner.

    The BSCMATRIX smart contract cannot be stopped, censored or controlled!

  • HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO START?

    BSCMATRIX.com costs just 0.05 BNB (Approx 12USD) to register - this enters you into both X3 and x6 Matrices. From here you can choose to upgrade each Matrix independently. From this point on you will be able to start building your Matrix, earning income and will also receive a lifetime income from the games Dividend payments.

  • HOW DOES BSCMATRIX COMPARE TO FORSAGE?

    BSCMATRIX is very similar to Forsage ~ but with 3 distinct differences:

    1: We offer dividends on top of your Partner Earnings! You will earn a lifetime dividend from BSCMATRIX!

    2: It's still early! Joining a game like BSCMATRIX early on gives massive benefits!

    3: BSCMATRIX runs on the incredibly efficient and cheap Binance Smart Chain! Wither low transaction/gas fees!

    We also belive BSCMATRIX is far easier to use, understand and therefore build your Matrix with!

  • WHICH WALLET CAN I USE?

    BSCMATRIX.com has been tested to be compatible with all major Web3 BSC wallets such as Trust Wallet, Metamask etc - including all major mobile wallets.

  • CAN I REGISTER WITHOUT A REFERRAL?

    Yes, you will be assigned under user ID: 1 and can then start to build your Matrix.

  • HOW DOES THE X3 MATRIX WORK?

    BSCMATRIX x3 has 12 levels, each level is twice the price of the previous. Each level has 3 Partners below you (And you are referred by another account):

    For your first 2 partners below you - their payments into the BSCMATRIX go directly into your wallet (Minus the Dividend commission of 2%).

    The 3rd partner that comes under your account at each level auto reinvests for you! The payment comes to you but auto-pays for the level to be renewed by paying your upstream partner (At the same level).

    This renewal process resets your current partner's connection.... at this point you should upgrade to the next level, if not the next round of payments at this level will be redirected to your upstream partner!

    You will also receive these “upstream/overflow” payments from your downstream partners too when they renew (See payment flow from User H)!

    ~ There are no expiry dates on partners or levels!

    ~ You can UPGRADE to each level at any stage - but must do so in sequence.

    Read more in the full BSCMATRIX Game Guide

  • HOW DOES THE x6 MATRIX WORK?

    BSCMATRIX x6 has 12 levels like x3, each level is twice the price of the previous. But unlike x3 this system is 2 levels deep for your referred partners

    Your first 2 partners under x6 pay their payments (Minus the Dividend commission) direct to your current partner (User A).

    The second level (Users E, F, G & H) are your payment level with Users E,F,G paying direct BNB directly into your wallet (Minus the dividend commission).

    User H is the auto-reinvest payment - renewing the level for you... as with the x3 system this clears your referrals letting the system begin again but only if you upgrade to the next level ~ otherwise the next round of payments from your partners would go up your matrix to your partner!

    ~ There are no expiry dates on partners or levels!

    ~ You can UPGRADE to each level at any stage - but must do so in sequence.

    Read more in the full BSCMATRIX Game Guide

  • HOW DO THE DIVIDENDS WORK?

    What makes BSCMATRIX unique from other games is our lifetime dividends system and TOKEN mining!

    From every matrix payment a tiny 2% fee is charged ~ this is not a dev payment but paid into the dividend pool which is distributed to everyone who holds and staked the $MATRIX token!

    The $MATRIX token is mined with every single play of the game at a fixed rate of 1 MATRIX for Level 1 with the reward doubling for each Level PURCHASED after!

    You continue to earn even if your matrix remains idle!

    The MATRIX token is also a full BEP20 token so can be swapped, traded or transferred between wallets like any other standard Binance token!

    Read more in the full BSCMATRIX Game Guide
